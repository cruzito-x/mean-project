To request a reversal of your purchase or product exchange, contact us at {{"admon@cinnatechstore.com"}}. You can exercise this right as long as you have not started using the product, which must be returned by the customer to the CinnaTech Store branch, C.C. Plaza Merliot, 3rd level local 308, Santa Tecla, La Libertad. In the email, provide your name, the purchased product, and the day you received the product. Then, let us know the reason for requesting the right of withdrawal.

  • Your purchase was made on our website https://cinnatechstore.com.
  • Your request was made within the first 8 days after receiving your product.
  • Present the original purchase ticket or invoice; if lost, the customer must provide their full name, purchase date, payment method, and place of purchase.
  • The product must not have been used and should be returned in good condition (without visible damage), with its original packaging elements (labels, seals, instructions, documents, warranty certificates, boxes, protective elements, software license codes not scratched, software license codes not activated).
  • The packaging (box) of the product must have its adhesive seals unaltered (not cut, removed, or peeled).
  • The product will be inspected by our technicians to validate its integrity and good condition before proceeding with the request.
  • The refund will be processed using the same payment method used for the purchase, handled by the corresponding institution.
  • If the payment method was a debit or credit card, a form from the corresponding financial institution will be provided through the email {{"admon@cinnatechstore.com"}} so the customer can process their refund. This form will be provided to the customer within a maximum of 15 days. The refund time depends on the financial institution of the payment method used for the purchase. If a month has passed and the refund has not been credited, the customer should contact the payment institution to check the status of the refund process.
- Payment Reversal

Based on the Consumer Protection Law (Art. 13-D), you may request a payment reversal/refund for purchases from our webstore under the following conditions:

  • Exercise your right of withdrawal.
  • The purchased product has not been received.
  • The received product does not match what was ordered.
  • The product has a manufacturing defect (see our RMA Warranty).
  • There are errors in the transaction charges due to third-party payment system failures.
  • If the payment method was a debit or credit card, a form from the corresponding financial institution will be provided through the email {{"admon@cinnatechstore.com"}} so the customer can process their refund. The refund time depends on the financial institution of the payment method used for the purchase.
