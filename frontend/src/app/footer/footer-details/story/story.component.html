CinnaTech Store

CinnaTech Store was born out of a dream, the dream of sharing and extending the technological commerce with everyone who wanted to keep up with the technological environment, regardless of age, ethnicity and so on.
Since our inception, we have always wanted to bring to the hands of every Salvadoran the smallest of gadgets, in order to always keep interconnected to each of our customers and consumers of our sponsoring brands.

Our team

Our team of technicians strives daily to provide the best service under the highest standards of quality in customer service issues, this has allowed us to push us more and more, allowing us to grow as a company and gain recognition thanks to the Salvadoran population.
All this from our venture in a store in the commercial area of Santa Tecla, thus taking off our dream of bringing interconnectivity to Salvadoran homes and allowing us to be the facilitators to solve their technological problems.
