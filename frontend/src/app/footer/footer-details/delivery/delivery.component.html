CinnaTech Store El Salvador has home delivery service throughout the country, however we are subject to the coverage of our carrier who is in charge of this service.

When buying in our Webstore the customer has the following options for the delivery of his order:

  • Pick up at store. To pick up in store, in which the buyer must select the branch of his preference within the list of available stores. Once the order has been processed, a Zona Digital customer service agent will contact the customer to schedule the day and time of pick up.

  • Home shipping. In which the information that the user has registered in our form for home deliveries will be taken to schedule the delivery through our carrier. If there is a discrepancy in the address, additional information or lack of coverage, a customer service agent will contact you to resolve the situation.
Shipping Cost

Home delivery includes an additional charge, which will be reflected in the detail of the order "My cart" according to the department or municipality selected.

Delivery Times

Home deliveries in the Metropolitan Areas of San Salvador and Santa Tecla have an estimated delivery time of 24 working hours; for deliveries to the interior of the country in the departmental capitals the estimated delivery time is 48 to 72 working hours and for some municipalities up to 5 working days.
